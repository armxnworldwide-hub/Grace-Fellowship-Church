Our Story

About Grace Fellowship Church

Grace Fellowship Church is a vibrant community in Ludhiana, Punjab, dedicated to advancing God's kingdom through worship, prayer, and spiritual growth. We believe in the transformative power of faith and the importance of fellowship in our Christian journey.

Founded on the principles of grace, we welcome all who seek to grow spiritually and find community in Christ. Our mission is to equip believers to become passionate followers of Jesus and effective witnesses in their communities.

Our Values

  • Faith: Trusting in God's Word and His promises
  • Community: Growing together in fellowship and love
  • Service: Serving others with compassion and grace
  • Growth: Continually maturing in our faith journey
  • Excellence: Giving our best in worship and ministry

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What time are Sunday services?

▼

Our Sunday Morning Worship service starts at 9:30 AM in the Main Sanctuary. Sunday School begins at 9:00 AM. We also offer live services online every night at 9:30 PM via Google Meet.

Do I need to dress up for church?

▼

Come as you are! We believe that worship is about your heart, not your clothing. Wear whatever makes you comfortable and allows you to focus on God.

Is there childcare available during services?

▼

Yes! We offer nursery care and Sunday School for children during our main service. Our trained volunteers provide a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn about Jesus.

What is your church's stance on giving?

▼

Giving is a personal decision. We believe tithing (10% of income) is a biblical practice, but we welcome any contribution, whether it's monetary or through volunteering your time and talents.

How can I get connected to a small group?

▼

We have various small groups meeting throughout the week including Bible studies, prayer groups, and fellowship groups. Visit the Welcome Desk on Sunday or contact our office for more information.

What if I'm not a Christian yet?

▼

You're welcome here! Grace Fellowship Church is a place where all people are welcome regardless of their faith background. We'd love to help you learn more about Jesus and God's love for you.
